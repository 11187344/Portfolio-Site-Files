In Unit 3 Digital Portfolio, we learned how to plan, create, and present a digital portfolio to show off our work and skills. The main goal of this unit is to build a professional-looking portfolio that could be used for things like applying for a college course, apprenticeship, or even a job in the future.
We used tools and software to build our portfolios, adding text, images, links, and examples of our best work from other units. We also made sure it was well presented, with consistent formatting and good visual design. Part of the unit involved testing our portfolio to make sure all the links worked and everything displayed correctly.
