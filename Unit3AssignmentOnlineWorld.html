I’m currently doing the Level 2 World IT course at college and so far it’s been a really good experience. The course covers a lot of useful topics like cybersecurity, computer hardware, software, data handling, and basic networking. I’ve already learned loads of new things that I didn’t know before.
Another great part of the course is the hands on work. We don’t just learn theory we actually get to work with real equipment and complete practical tasks, which makes everything make more sense. It’s helped me feel more confident using computers and understanding how IT works in real life.
I’m glad I decided to do this course, because it’s helping me build useful skills for the future. It’s also given me a better idea of what it’s like to work in IT. I feel more prepared now to move on to the next level or even think about a job in the tech industry. If you’re interested in IT or just want to get better with computers.
